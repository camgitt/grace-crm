## Getting Started

### First Login

1. Navigate to your GRACE CRM URL
2. Sign in with your credentials (or continue in Demo Mode)
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard** - your mission control

### Understanding the Navigation

The left sidebar contains all main sections:

| Icon | Section | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| Home | Dashboard | Your daily action feed |
| Users | People | Member directory & profiles |
| CheckSquare | Tasks | Follow-ups & to-dos |
| Calendar | Calendar | Events & services |
| Users | Small Groups | Group management |
| ClipboardCheck | Attendance | Check-in & tracking |
| Heart | Prayer | Prayer request board |
| DollarSign | Giving | Donation management |
| Settings | Settings | App configuration |

## Dashboard

The Dashboard is designed around **action, not information**. It shows you exactly what needs attention right now.

### Action Feed

Items are organized by urgency:

**URGENT (Red)**
- New visitors within the last 7 days
- Overdue tasks

**TODAY (Blue)**
- Tasks due today
- Birthdays today

**COMING UP (Gray)**
- Inactive members needing care
- Tasks due in the next 3 days

### Quick Stats

Small badges at the top show:
- Total people in your database
- New visitors (amber)
- Overdue tasks (red)
- Pending tasks (blue)

### Analytics

Click **"Analytics & Trends"** at the bottom to expand charts showing:
- Member growth over time
- Status distribution
- Engagement metrics

## People Management

### Viewing People

Access via **People** in the sidebar. You'll see:
- Search bar for finding anyone
- Filter buttons (All, Visitors, Members, etc.)
- List of all people with quick info

### Person Statuses

| Status | Meaning | Action Needed |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| Visitor | First-time or recent guest | Follow up within 48 hours |
| Regular | Attends consistently | Invite to membership |
| Member | Official member | Engage in serving/groups |
| Leader | Serving in ministry | Support & develop |
| Inactive | Not seen recently | Reach out with care |

### Adding a New Person

1. Click the **+** button or use Quick Actions
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Required**: First name, Last name
   - **Recommended**: Email, Phone, Status
   - **Optional**: Birthday, Address, Tags, Notes
3. Click **Save**

### Person Profile

Click any person to see their full profile:

**Overview Tab**
- Contact information
- Status and tags
- Quick action buttons (Email, SMS, Call)

**Timeline Tab**
- All interactions logged
- Notes and follow-ups
- Attendance history

**Groups Tab**
- Small groups they belong to
- Leadership roles

### Bulk Actions

Select multiple people using checkboxes:
- **Change Status**: Move visitors to members, etc.
- **Add Tags**: Apply labels like "New Here" or "Volunteer"
- **Export**: Download as CSV

### Importing People

1. Click the import icon
2. Upload a CSV file with columns:
   - firstName, lastName, email, phone, status
3. Review the import preview
4. Confirm to add all records

## Tasks & Follow-Ups

### Task Types

| Category | Color | Examples |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Follow-up | Blue | Call new visitor, Check on member |
| Care | Pink | Hospital visit, Bereavement |
| Admin | Gray | Update records, Prepare report |
| Outreach | Green | Community event, Evangelism |

### Creating a Task

1. Go to **Tasks** section
2. Click **+ Add Task**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title**: What needs to be done
   - **Person**: Who it's about (optional)
   - **Due Date**: When it's due
   - **Priority**: High, Medium, or Low
   - **Category**: Type of task
4. Save

### Priority Levels

| Priority | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| High | Do today/ASAP |
| Medium | Do this week |
| Low | Do when possible |

### Recurring Tasks

Check "Recurring" when creating a task:
- When completed, a new task is automatically created
- Great for weekly check-ins, monthly reviews

### Completing Tasks

- Click the checkbox to mark complete
- Completed tasks move to the "Completed" tab
- Recurring tasks generate a new instance

## Calendar & Events

### Event Types

| Type | Color | Examples |
|------|-------|----------|
| Service | Blue | Sunday worship, Midweek service |
| Meeting | Purple | Board meeting, Team huddle |
| Event | Green | Potluck, Concert, Conference |
| Holiday | Orange | Christmas, Easter, Special days |

### Creating an Event

1. Go to **Calendar**
2. Click on a date or use **+ Add Event**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title**: Event name
   - **Type**: Service, Meeting, Event, or Holiday
   - **Date & Time**: When it happens
   - **Location**: Where (optional)
   - **Description**: Details (optional)
4. Save

### RSVP Tracking

For events with RSVPs:
- Click an event to see RSVP status
- View who's attending, maybe, or declined
- Track headcount for planning

### Calendar Views

Switch between:
- **Month View**: See the full month
- **Week View**: Detailed weekly schedule
- **List View**: Upcoming events in a list

## Small Groups

### What Are Small Groups?

Small groups are the backbone of community:
- Bible studies
- Life groups
- Serve teams
- Interest groups

### Creating a Small Group

1. Go to **Small Groups**
2. Click **+ New Group**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name**: Group name
   - **Leader**: Who leads it
   - **Meeting Day**: When they meet
   - **Meeting Time**: What time
   - **Location**: Where they meet
   - **Description**: What the group is about
4. Save

### Managing Members

In a group's detail view:
- **Add Members**: Search and add people
- **Remove Members**: Click X next to a name
- **Change Leader**: Assign a new leader

### Group Statistics

Each group shows:
- Total members
- Average attendance
- Recent meeting history

## Prayer Requests

### Viewing Requests

The prayer board shows:
- **Active Requests**: Current prayer needs
- **Answered Prayers**: Testimonies of answered prayer

### Submitting a Request

1. Go to **Prayer**
2. Click **+ New Request**
3. Fill in:
   - **Request**: What to pray for
   - **Person**: Who it's for (optional)
   - **Visibility**: Public or Private
4. Submit

### Marking Answered

When God answers:
1. Click the request
2. Click **Mark as Answered**
3. Optionally add a testimony

### Privacy Levels

| Level | Who Can See |
|-------|-------------|
| Public | All staff members |
| Private | Only you and admins |

## Giving & Donations

### Recording a Donation

1. Go to **Giving**
2. Click **+ Record Gift**
3. Enter:
   - **Donor**: Who gave
   - **Amount**: How much
   - **Date**: When
   - **Fund**: Where it goes (General, Missions, etc.)
   - **Method**: Cash, Check, Card, Online
4. Save

### Giving Dashboard

See at a glance:
- Total giving this period
- Giving by fund
- Recent transactions
- Top contributors (admin only)

### Recurring Giving

For pledges and recurring gifts:
- Track committed amounts
- See fulfillment status
- Send reminders

### Generating Statements

For tax purposes:
1. Go to a donor's profile
2. Click **Generate Statement**
3. Select date range
4. Download or email PDF

For more details, see [Giving System Guide](./GIVING_SYSTEM_GUIDE.md).

## Communication Tools

### Sending Email

From a person's profile:
1. Click the **Email** button
2. Compose your message
3. Send

Email templates available for common messages.

### Sending SMS

From a person's profile:
1. Click the **SMS** button
2. Type your message (160 chars)
3. Send

Requires Twilio integration to be configured.

### Bulk Communication

From the People list:
1. Select multiple people
2. Click **Send Message**
3. Choose Email or SMS
4. Compose and send

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"I can't see certain features"**
- Your role may not have permission
- Contact your admin to adjust access

**"Search isn't finding someone"**
- Try searching by first name only
- Check for typos in the original record

**"SMS isn't sending"**
- Verify Twilio is configured in Settings
- Check the phone number format

**"Giving totals seem wrong"**
- Verify date range filter
- Check that all donations have the correct fund assigned

### Getting Help

- **Documentation**: Check this manual first
- **Support**: Contact your administrator
- **Bugs**: Report at github.com/camgitt/grace-crm/issues

## Best Practices

### For Visitor Follow-Up

1. **Within 24 hours**: Send a thank-you email
2. **Within 48 hours**: Make a personal call
3. **Within 1 week**: Invite to small group or event
4. **Track everything**: Log all interactions

### For Data Hygiene

- Update statuses regularly
- Merge duplicate records
- Archive truly inactive members (don't delete)
- Back up data monthly

### For Team Collaboration

- Assign tasks to specific people
- Use tags consistently
- Add notes to profiles liberally
- Check the dashboard daily

## Appendix: Status Workflow

```
┌──────────┐     ┌──────────┐     ┌──────────┐     ┌──────────┐
│  Visitor │ ──► │  Regular │ ──► │  Member  │ ──► │  Leader  │
└──────────┘     └──────────┘     └──────────┘     └──────────┘
      │                                │
      │                                │
      ▼                                ▼
┌──────────┐                    ┌──────────┐
│  (left)  │                    │ Inactive │
└──────────┘                    └──────────┘
```

**Transition Triggers:**
- Visitor → Regular: Attends 3+ times
- Regular → Member: Completes membership class
- Member → Leader: Assigned ministry role
- Any → Inactive: No attendance for 60+ days
